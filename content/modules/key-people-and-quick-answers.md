## How to Use This Page

This is the page you'll come back to more than any other. Key contacts, system URLs, attendance thresholds, SOP numbers, file naming rules — it's all here in one place. Don't try to memorize everything. Just know where to find it.

Bookmark it. Print it. Keep it open in a tab. Whatever works for you — just make sure it's within reach, because you're going to need it.

## Numbers You Should Know

These are the values that come up constantly — in conversations with employees, during payroll, and in your own daily work. You don't need to memorize all of them on day one, but by day 30 the most common ones should be second nature.

### Pay Period & Payroll

- **Pay period:** Biweekly (Sunday through the following Saturday)
- **Payroll deadline:** Tuesday at 6:00 PM CST — no exceptions
- **File naming standard:** `YYYY-MM-DD DOC NAME - FIRST NAME LAST NAME`

### Attendance Points

| Situation | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Less than 5 minutes late (grace period) | 0.0 |
| Tardy or left early — less than half a shift | 0.5 |
| Full shift absence | 1.0 |
| No-call or late call (15+ minutes past shift start) | 1.5 |

### Corrective Action Thresholds

| Points | Action |
|--------|--------|
| 5.0 | Coaching conversation |
| 6.0 | Verbal warning |
| 7.0 | Written warning |
| 8.0 | Termination |

### PTO Increments

- **Vacation:** 2-hour minimum increments
- **Personal time:** 1-hour minimum increments

### Drug Screening

- **Window:** 3 business days from signed offer letter
- **Cost to candidate:** Free
- **Requirement:** Photo ID
- **Rule:** Never confirm a start date until results are back

### Benefits Timeline

| Milestone | What Becomes Available |
|-----------|----------------------|
| 60 days of employment | PTO eligible (personal time, vacation accrual) |
| 1st of the month after 60 days | Medical, dental, vision, 401(k) enrollment |
| 365 days + 1,250 hours worked | FMLA eligible |

### FMLA

- **Allowance:** 480 hours per year
- **Paid or unpaid:** Unpaid
- **Your role:** Escalate to the HR Manager immediately — do not advise or interpret eligibility
- **Confidentiality:** FMLA status is confidential from everyone, including supervisors

### 401(k) Match

- **Formula:** 100% match on the first 3% + 50% match on the next 2%
