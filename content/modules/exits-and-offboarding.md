## Separation Overview

When an employee leaves AAP — whether voluntarily or involuntarily — there are specific steps to ensure systems are updated, final pay is handled correctly, and compliance is maintained.

## Final Pay & Benefits at Separation

| Benefit | At Separation |
|---------|--------------|
| Accrued vacation | **Paid out** |
| Personal leave | **Forfeited** — does not carry over or pay out |
| Long-term sick leave | **Relinquished** |

## System Removal

- Remove employee from all HR systems
- Notify IT — they handle computer access, email, and network credentials
- Remove from Proton Pass vault if the departing employee had HR system access

## Voluntary Separations

- Employee provides notice (typically 2 weeks)
- HR processes final pay, benefits termination, and exit steps
- Manager collects company property (badge, keys, laptop, equipment)

## Involuntary Separations

:::callout warning
Involuntary terminations are handled by the HR Manager only. Never communicate a termination yourself.
:::

- HR Manager leads the process
- Your role: support documentation, system updates, and filing as directed

## 2-Day No-Call/No-Show

**2 consecutive days without reporting in = voluntary quit without notice.**

Notify the HR Manager immediately when this occurs.
