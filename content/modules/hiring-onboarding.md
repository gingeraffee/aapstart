## Hiring & Onboarding

After offer acceptance, the manager's speed and follow-through set the tone for the employee experience.

## Before Day One

- Confirm start date, schedule, and reporting structure with HR.
- Ensure workspace, system access, and role tools are ready.
- Share first-week plan with clear priorities.
- Coordinate onboarding tasks in BambooHR with HR.

## First Week Manager Expectations

- Hold a day-one welcome and role expectations conversation.
- Schedule required training and key introductions.
- Set check-ins for week one and week two.
- Confirm employee understands who to contact for support.
