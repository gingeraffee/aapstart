## Purpose of This Guide

This guide outlines the steps hiring managers must follow to prepare a new hire for their first day at AAP and API. From completing the Position Request in BambooHR to coordinating IT access, facilities, equipment, and department-specific needs, this process helps to support compliance, accuracy, and a smooth onboarding experience across all AAP/API divisions.

## Confirm Offer Acceptance and HR Status Update

After the written offer is signed, HR will send the candidate the required Background Check Authorization form to complete. Once completed, HR will update the candidate's status in BambooHR to **Future Employee in Process**.

- The Hiring Manager will receive an automated BambooHR notification when the status changes.
- Background check and drug screen processing begins after the authorization form is completed and the status is updated.
- Onboarding begins only after HR confirms the status update to **Future Employee in Process**.

:::callout warning "Follow up if needed"
If you have not received the BambooHR notification within 1 business day, contact HR.
:::

## Open the Position Request in BambooHR

- Log in to BambooHR.
- Search for the future employee's name.
- Select the future employee under **Employees**, not Job Candidates.
- Open the **Position Request** tab. It may appear under the **More** tab.
- Review the **Onboarding Process Reference Guide** before completing the request.

## New Employee Setup Information

Complete the following fields in the Position Request:

- Position Request Type
- Position/Title
- Division (AAP or API)
- Office Location
- Reporting Manager
- Preferred Start Date
- Preferred Display Name -- Used in BambooHR, internal directories, and, if applicable, the employee's email display name/signature. Example: Mike McNair instead of Michael McNair.
- New Employee Setup Notes

:::callout info "What happens next"
Submitting this request triggers notifications to HR, IT, Marketing, Facilities, and Division Leads to begin setup and onboarding actions.
:::

## Complete the Pre-Boarding Request

:::callout warning
**Important:** Request only the items needed for Day 1 or within the first 30 days of the role.
:::

**Company Cell Phone --** Company-issued phone/number.
**Request when:** The role is on-call, requires frequent travel, requires MFA or authentication apps for work, or must be reachable after hours.
**Examples:** Managers, IT, sales, and delivery-related leadership roles.

**Company Credit Card --** Company purchasing and travel expenses on an assigned card.
**Request when:** The employee will book travel, pay vendors, purchase supplies, or regularly incur reimbursable business expenses.
**Examples:** Managers, directors, and employees with regular purchases or travel responsibilities.
**Note:** Approval required.

**Company Vehicle or Transfer --** Assign a company vehicle or transfer an existing vehicle assignment.
**Request when:** The role requires regular travel between sites, deliveries, vendor visits, or frequent offsite work.
**Examples:** Delivery-related roles, multi-site leaders, or positions with regular field travel.
**Note:** Approval required.

**AAA Coverage --** Roadside assistance coverage for a company vehicle or required-travel role.
**Request when:** The employee is assigned a company vehicle or has frequent business driving requirements.

**Workstation Type (Desktop or Laptop) --** Hardware setup for the employee.
**Request when:**
- **Desktop:** Primarily onsite with a fixed workspace.
- **Laptop:** Hybrid or remote work, regular travel, or frequent meetings across sites.

*IT will confirm model and standard equipment.*

**Concur Access --** Travel and expense reporting access.
**Request when:** The employee will travel or submit business expenses.

**Business Cards --** Printed or digital business cards.
**Request when:** The role is customer-facing or vendor-facing, or includes recruiting, community outreach, sales, or leadership responsibilities.
**Examples:** Sales, leadership, recruiting, and other external-facing roles.

**Marketing Introduction --** Internal or external welcome announcement support.
**Request when:** The role is a leadership role, a customer-facing position, or when the department or work location would like a formal introduction shared.
**Examples:** Leadership roles, customer-facing positions, or high-visibility new hires.

### Enter the Onboarding Request

**Building key** -- Physical key for the building/exterior access where applicable.

**Office key** -- Key for a specific office, room, cabinet, or secured interior space.
*Request when:* employee has an assigned private office or secured workspace.

**Badge / door access** -- Badge credentials and electronic access permissions.
*Request when:* needed for any site requiring controlled entry; specify areas/doors if role-restricted.

:::callout info
Submitting these will automatically route a request to Facilities for setup based on location.
:::

### Enter the Warehouse Onboarding Request

*For warehouse roles only, if applicable.*

- Select warehouse role type.
- Choose warehouse location.
- Request AS400 or S2K system access.

### Enter the IT Account Setup Requirements

*If applicable.*

- Active Directory and email setup.
- AS400, S2K, Adobe, Dynamics, MapiLab.
- Zoom phone, website access, VPN.
- Remote hire equipment shipment.
- Additional IT needs notes.
- Shipping information for equipment (if necessary).

## Review & Submit the Position Request

- Submit the completed form.
- BambooHR triggers an IT ticket and all internal workflows.

:::callout warning
Double-check all fields before submitting. Incorrect entries can delay IT setup and access provisioning.
:::

## Coordinate First-Day Details (Pre-Start)

- Confirm start date/time, location, and supervisor with HR (and the employee).
- Workspace and equipment ready: desk, badge/keys, laptop/desktop, headset/monitors if needed -- or confirm remote delivery.
- Orientation plan and schedule: send the employee a Day 1 agenda (who/where/when).
- Team introductions: notify team of the start date and who will support onboarding/training.

## First Day Expectations

- Welcome the new employee (quick tour and introductions).
- Review the Day 1 schedule and what "success" looks like this week.
- Confirm access is working: email, BambooHR, required systems, and shared drives.
- Cover essentials: work hours/breaks, attendance expectations, safety basics, communication norms.
- Start role training: job overview, priorities, key processes, and who to ask for help.
