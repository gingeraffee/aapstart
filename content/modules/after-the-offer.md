## New Hire BambooHR Onboarding (HRA-04)

**Before you start:** Confirm pre-hire screenings are received and reviewed (background, drug screen, reference checks). Confirm start date, location, division, status, and department. Verify the employee's state for tax form requirements.

**Step-by-step (HRA-04):**

1. Applicant Tracking → click **Hire** from the Offer Tracking section
2. **Uncheck** the box to close the job posting
3. Click **Add New Employee** → select the checkbox to send a new hire packet
4. Enter employee number as **TBA[Hire Date]**
5. Enter location, division, status, department, compensation, and overtime amounts → click Continue/Next
6. Select the **applicable template** based on location, department, and status
7. Click **Add Onboarding Tasks** to auto-fill tasks by department, status, and location
8. **Check the employee's state:** If NOT in Tennessee or Alabama, manually add the state tax form and mark it **Required**
9. Preview and send the new hire paperwork
10. Complete your assigned onboarding tasks on or before the due date — mark complete
11. Follow up on the employee page as documents are signed (you'll receive emails as each is completed)
12. **File signed documents in the correct BambooHR folders:**
    - New hire forms → *New Hire Forms folder*
    - AAP Confidential Medical form → *Medical Documents*
    - ACH, W-4, state tax forms → *Payroll Related Documents*
    - I-9 documentation (signed I-9 + two forms of ID) → *I-9 Folder (restricted)*

:::callout warning
Escalate if: start date/status/compensation/location changes after hire entry; state tax form cannot be identified; onboarding docs incomplete/past due; I-9 missing or invalid; any compliance concern.
:::

## Introductory Period & Benefits at Hire

**Introductory Period:** First 60 days. Max **2 attendance points** allowed. No vacation accrual yet (personal time eligibility begins at 60 days).

**After 60 days (regular full-time):** Personal time eligible; vacation accrual begins; holiday pay; health insurance enrollment on 1st of month after 60 days; 401(k) enrollment on 1st of month after 60 days.

## At Separation

- Accrued vacation → **paid out**
- Personal leave → **forfeited**
- Long-term sick leave → **relinquished**
- Remove employee from all systems; notify IT (they handle computer access); remove from Proton Pass vault if HR staff

:::callout warning
Involuntary terminations handled by HR Manager only. Never communicate a termination yourself.
:::

**2-Day No-Call/No-Show = Voluntary quit.** Notify HR Manager immediately.
