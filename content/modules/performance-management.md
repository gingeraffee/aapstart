## Manager BambooHR Performance Management Guide

This guide explains how managers should use BambooHR Performance Management to evaluate employees, provide meaningful feedback, support development, and ensure consistency across AAP/API.

## 1. Overview of BambooHR Performance Management

- Centralized tool for assessments, goals, 1:1s, and development tracking.
- Supports continuous performance conversations instead of once-a-year reviews.
- Helps managers identify strengths, opportunities, and engagement needs.

## 2. Components of BambooHR Performance

- **Manager Assessments** — Structured evaluation of role expectations and performance behaviors.
- **Self-Assessments** — Employees reflect on their performance and goals.
- **Peer Assessments** (if enabled) — Optional peer feedback to broaden insight.
- **Goals** — Track progress on quarterly or annual goals.
- **1:1 Meetings** — Scheduled conversations documented directly in BambooHR.

## 3. Performance Review Cycle

1. BambooHR will notify managers when review cycles open.
2. Managers complete assessments by due dates.
3. Employees complete self-assessments before manager review.
4. Once both are complete, schedule a performance meeting.
5. Finalize review and submit acknowledgement.

## 4. Completing Manager Assessments

:::checklist
- [ ] Review employee job description and core expectations.
- [ ] Rate each performance area according to standards.
- [ ] Provide examples that support ratings.
- [ ] Focus on behaviors and results, not personality.
- [ ] Identify strengths and development opportunities.
:::

## 5. Conducting 1:1 Performance Meetings

1. Schedule a private meeting at least 30–60 minutes.
2. Review the employee's self-assessment first.
3. Share your assessment with clarity and support.
4. Use specific examples and focus on behaviors.
5. Allow the employee to ask questions and respond.
6. Set expectations for the next review cycle.

## 6. Setting Employee Goals in BambooHR

- Goals should be **SMART** (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
- Align goals with departmental objectives and company vision.
- Enter goals in BambooHR to track progress.
- Review goals during monthly or quarterly check-ins.
- Update status regularly to maintain accountability.

## 7. Using 1:1 Tools in BambooHR

- Create recurring or one-time 1:1 meetings.
- Use talking points and discussion templates.
- Document action items and follow-ups.
- Both manager and employee can add agenda items.
- Document conversations for compliance and consistency.

## 8. Calibration and Fair Performance Ratings

- Apply consistent standards across similar roles.
- Compare employee performance with expectations — not each other.
- Avoid bias by using facts, examples, and documented behaviors.

## 9. Legal and Compliance Considerations

:::callout warning
Documentation in BambooHR may be referenced during unemployment or legal claims. All comments must be factual, job-related, and based solely on performance results and behaviors.
:::

- Maintain factual, job-related documentation.
- Avoid comments related to medical, personal, or protected-class information.
- Comments must be based solely on performance results and behaviors.
- Document improvement needs clearly to support coaching or PIP processes.

## 10. Linking Performance to Coaching and Development

- Use performance reviews to guide future learning and development.
- Identify training needs or skill gaps.
- Connect performance concerns to coaching conversations.
- If serious issues exist, consider a PIP with HR guidance.

## 11. Manager Expectations

:::checklist
- [ ] Complete all reviews by deadlines.
- [ ] Provide specific, actionable feedback.
- [ ] Meet with each employee to discuss results.
- [ ] Maintain professionalism, support growth, and partner with HR as needed.
:::
