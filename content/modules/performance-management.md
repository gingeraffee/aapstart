## Purpose of This Guide

This guide explains how managers should use BambooHR Performance Management to complete reviews, hold meaningful performance conversations, set goals, document 1:1s , and support employee development across AAP/API.

## 1. Overview of BambooHR Performance Management

The BambooHR Performance Management tool is a critical part of employee development. Mangers utilizing this tool will find their employees are more engaged and aware of their strengths and weaknesses.

**Performance Management benefits:**

- Centralized tool for performance reviews, goals, 1:1 meetings, and development tracking.
- Supports ongoing performance conversations, not just once-a-year reviews.
- Helps managers identify strengths, development opportunities, and engagement needs.

## 2. Components of BambooHR Performance

BambooHR Performance Management includes several tools managers may use throughout the review proce4ss:

- **Manager Assessments** — Structured evaluation of role expectations and performance behaviors.
- **Self-Assessments** — Employees reflect on their performance and goals.
- **Peer Assessments** (if enabled) — Optional peer feedback to broaden insight.
- **Goals (if enabled)** — Track progress on quarterly or annual goals.
- **1:1 Meetings (if enabled)** — Ongoing conversations documented directly in BambooHR.

## 3. Performance Review Cycle

BambooHR will notify managers when a review cycle opens. Employees complete self-assessments first, and managers complete their assessments by the assigned due date. Once both are complete, managers should schedule a performance discussion, review results with the employee, and finalize the review acknowledgement.

## 4. Completing Manager Assessments

When completing a manager assessment:

- Review the employee's job description and core expectations
- Rate each performance area according to the established standards
- Provide specific examples that support each rating
- Focus on behaviors and results, not personality
- Identify both strengths and development opportunities

## 5. Conducting 1:1 Performance Meetings

Schedule a private meeting lasting approximately 30-60 minutes. Review the employee's self-assessment in advance, then share your assessment clearly and supportively. Use specific examples, focus on behaviors and results, allow time for questions, and set expectations for the next review cylce.


## 6. Setting Employee Goals in BambooHR

Employee goals should be SMART: Specific, Measureable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. Goals should align with departmental priorities and company objectives. Enter goals into BambooHR for tracking and they should bne reviewed during regular check-ins. Managers should update goal status as needed to maintain accountability.

## 7. Using 1:1 Tools in BambooHR

Managers can use BambooHR to create recurring or one-time 1:1 meetings, organize talking points, document action items, and track follow-up discussions. Both the manager and employee can add agenda items, helping create more meaningful and consistent conversations. 

## 8. Calibration and Fair Performance Ratings

Managers should apply consistent standards across similar roles and evaluate employees again role expectations - not again one another. Use facts, examples, and documented behaviors to support ratings and reduce bias.

## 9. Legal and Compliance Considerations

Performance documentation in BambooHR may later be referenced in unemployment matters, employee relations reviews, or legal claims. All comments should remain factual, job-related, and based only on performance results and observed behaviors.

:::callout warning
- Do not include medical, personal, opr protected-class information
- Keep documentation factual and professional
- Clearly document improvement needs when follow-up coaching or further action may be needed
:::

## 10. Linking Performance to Coaching and Development

 Performance reviews should be used to guide employee growth and future development. Managers should identify skill gaps, discuss training needs, and connect performance concerns to coaching conversations. If more serious issue exist, a Performance Improvement Plan should be considered with HR guidance.

## 11. Manager Expectations

Managers are expected to:

- Complete all reviews by the required deadlines
- Provide specific, actionable feedback
- Meet with each employee to discuss results
- Maintain professionalism, support employee growth, and partner with HR when needed
