## Daily Workflow

**Morning:** Check email/voicemail/calendar | Poll timeclocks in PayClock (PC-03) | Review BambooHR onboarding tasks | Triage employee questions per HRA-01

**Midday:** Follow up on overdue onboarding tasks | Follow up on missed punches / timecard exceptions | Prepare materials for HR Manager

**End of Day:** File documents within 48 hours | Update trackers (onboarding, attendance, recruiting) | Confirm tomorrow's interviews are ready

## Weekly, Monthly & 30/60/90 Plan

**Weekly:** Poll timeclocks → run BambooHR Time Off Used report (PC-05) → enter PTO → supervisor follow-ups on exceptions (CS-01) → final audit → Payroll Change Report (BH-02) → report to HR Manager → submit by Tuesday 6:00 PM CST.

**Monthly:** Perfect attendance update + summary report | Digital filing audit | Confirm templates and SOPs are current.

---

**Days 1–30:** System access setup | Learn folder structure and naming | Shadow PayClock audit | Learn BambooHR onboarding templates | Learn Employvio workflows

**Days 31–60:** Independently run weekly PayClock audit (PC-05) | Maintain recruiting coordination and filing without reminders

**Days 61–90:** Identify recurring issues and propose fixes | Refresh SOPs based on real experience

## Ownership Boundaries

**You own:** Timekeeping audits, PTO entry, onboarding task follow-up, recruiting scheduling, background screen initiation, digital filing, basic policy questions, first-point-of-contact employee inquiries (HRA-01), corrective action filing (CA-01).

**IT owns:** New hire computer privileges and licenses.

**HR Manager owns:** Pay decisions, involuntary separations, discipline, investigations, screening results, FMLA/leave/medical, benefits exceptions, legal language, safety incidents, harassment/discrimination, corrective action guidance, anything high-risk.

---

**Documentation rule (CS-02):** Document every employee interaction — date/time, method, what was asked, what was said, and any follow-up or escalation. Follow up by the committed date. If timelines change, update the employee promptly.
