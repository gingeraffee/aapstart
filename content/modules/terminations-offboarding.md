## Terminations & Offboarding

Separation processes must be handled with consistency, professionalism, and documented coordination with HR.

## Manager Responsibilities

- Engage HR before final action and align on process.
- Document objective facts and timeline clearly.
- Conduct separation conversations professionally and respectfully.
- Coordinate return of company property and access removal.

## Offboarding Handoff

- Confirm final day logistics.
- Ensure IT/access offboarding is initiated.
- Route payroll/benefits questions to HR.
- Notify relevant stakeholders on a need-to-know basis.

:::callout tip
Use a checklist for every offboarding event. Consistency protects the employee experience and reduces compliance risk.
:::
