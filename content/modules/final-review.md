## Before You Start the Quiz

A few things to keep in mind:

**Trust your training.** You've spent real time in these modules. The scenarios in this quiz are designed to test judgment, not trick you. If you've been doing the work, you'll recognize the situations.

**Think like you're on the job.** Every question is something that could actually happen. When you read a scenario, picture yourself at your desk. What would you actually do? That instinct is usually right.

**Watch for the nuance.** Most questions have two or three options that look reasonable. The difference between a good answer and the right answer is usually about boundaries — what's yours to handle vs. what gets routed, and how fast.

**You can always come back.** If a question stumps you, that's a sign you should revisit that module after. The quiz isn't the end of learning — it's a checkpoint.
