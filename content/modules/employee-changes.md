## Employee Changes

Employee updates must be handled quickly, accurately, and with clear documentation.

## Common Change Types

- Title or role updates
- Department or reporting changes
- Schedule or location changes
- Pay-related changes requiring HR/payroll coordination

## Manager Responsibilities

- Submit complete change details early.
- Coordinate timing with HR and payroll.
- Communicate clearly with the employee on effective date and expectations.
- Document approvals and supporting notes.

:::callout warning
Verbal approval is not enough. Changes must be documented in the approved workflow before they are considered final.
:::
